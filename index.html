FAQ

Got questions?

Do I need to download an app?
Nope! It works in any browser and can be saved to your iPhone home screen so it feels exactly like a real app. No App Store, no downloads, no updates to manage.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything is stored locally on your device — your expenses, passwords, notes, habits — none of it is sent to a server. You can also export a full backup anytime.
Does it work if I don't use Canvas?
Absolutely. Canvas sync is optional — if you don't use it, you can still manually add assignments. Everything else in the dashboard works without it.
What does "custom changes" mean?
Things like renaming sections, adding a new page, changing colors or layouts, adding features specific to you — basically anything you want changed. Just send me a message describing what you want and I'll make it happen.
How do I pay?
Email me and I'll send you a payment link. I accept Venmo, CashApp, and PayPal. After payment you'll get your dashboard link within 24 hours.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes, anytime. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel — just shoot me a message. No contracts, no commitments.
